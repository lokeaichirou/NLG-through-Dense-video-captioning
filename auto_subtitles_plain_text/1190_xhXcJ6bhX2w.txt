 hello and welcome to soul food cooking today I will be making a mac and cheese rafi in your oven to 350 degrees you will need 1 pound of macaroni 1 can of evaporated milk 1 stick of butter 1/4 cup of provolone cheese 3/4 cup of Muenster cheese 3/4 cups of sharp cheddar 3/4 cup of mild cheddar 3/4 cups of Monterey Jack 3/4 cup of Velveeta 2 eggs 1 teaspoon of black pepper 2 and 1/2 teaspoons of salt and 1 tablespoon of oil to your water add 1 teaspoon of salt and 1 tablespoon of oil bring water to a boil then add macaroni allow to cook for seven minutes stirring occasionally remove from heat and drain transfer a macaroni to pan then add butter and mix well you next incorporate evaporated milk now incorporate salt and pepper next incorporate eggs after six cheese's and mix well transfer pan to preheated oven bake for 15 minutes then stir to incorporate Jesus now allow to bake for an additional 20 to 25 minutes if you want to be a hit at your next family gathering then this macaroni and cheese recipe is definitely the dish to serve thanks for watching and don't forget to subscribe to soul food cooking
