 a recipe for a classic macaroni salad is a must-have during the summer start by cooking one box of elbow macaroni according to the package directions drain rinse and cool then add to a large bowl now combine 1 cup of mayonnaise two tablespoons of vinegar one tablespoon of Dijon mustard one teaspoon of sugar one teaspoon of salt and 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper to the same bowl add 1 cup thinly sliced celery 1 cup of chopped green and red peppers and a quarter cup of chopped red onions you this is a classic side to have at an outdoor barbecue and it can be made in advance to avoid any last-minute shuffle classic macaroni salad for more delicious summer recipes visit summer kitchen TV
