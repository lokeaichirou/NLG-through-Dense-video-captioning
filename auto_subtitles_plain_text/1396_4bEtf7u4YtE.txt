 Allrecipes member lauryn NJ says that Mary's meatloaf is the best meatloaf recipe I have tried to date my husband even loves it and he used to hate meatloaf thanks to all recipes member mary robinson for sharing this recipe begin by preheating your oven to 350 degrees next lightly grease a 9 by 5 inch loaf pan with cooking spray chopped 1 large onion cut 6 slices of white bread into cubes add one and a half pounds of lean ground beef to a large bowl along with the chopped onion season with salt round black pepper and garlic powder to taste mix this together now add in your cubed bread a half a cup of milk and one egg and mix these together next add two tablespoons of yellow mustard and 1/4 of a cup of ketchup mix this all together to incorporate all the ingredients place the mixture into your prepared loaf pan and spread out evenly press the meat to make sure it's compacted fully into the pan now you'll need to prepare the topping in a small bowl add 1 tbsp of yellow mustard a quarter cup of ketchup and three tablespoons of brown sugar stir to combine pour this mixture over your meatloaf you and spread it evenly over the top bake the meatloaf in the preheated oven for two hours or until the internal temperature reaches 165 degrees we measured on a thermometer Mary's meatloaf thanks again Mary
